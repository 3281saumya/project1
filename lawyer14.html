Advocate Kuldeep Singh



Advocate Kuldeep Singh possesses a thorough knowledge of the legal field and works with a result-oriented and professional approach. He is ethical in his outlook and offers consultation across a wide variety of legal matters.

Advocate Kuldeep specialises in both criminal legal defence and civil matters such as property disputes, family disputes, matrimonial matters, among others. He also handles consumer protection cases.

Advocate Kuldeep enrolled with the Bar Council of Uttar Pradesh in 2006. He is a member of the Uttar Pradesh Court Bar Association.
