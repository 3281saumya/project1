Advocate Vijay Singh

Advocate Vijay Singh has been practicing and handling cases independently with a result oriented approach, both professionally and ethically and has now acquired excellent professional experience in providing legal consultancy and advisory services.

Advocate Vijay provides services in various fields of Family Matters, Divorce Matters, Civil Matters, Child Custody Matters, Tax Matters and drafting and vetting of various agreements and documents.

Advocate Vijay enrolled with the Bar Council of Uttar Pradesh in 1994. He is a member of Rasulabad Bar Association.
