Advocate Radha Krishna Pandey



Advocate Radha Krishna Pandey has been practicing and handling cases independently with a result-oriented approach, both professionally and ethically and has now acquired many years of professional experience in providing legal consultancy and advisory services.

He provides services in the various fields of law, that is, Arbitration, Family, Landlord/Tenant, Criminal, Wills/Trusts, Civil, Labour & Service, Banking/Finance, Corporate and drafting and vetting of various agreements and documents.

Advocate Radha Krishna enrolled with the Bar Council of Uttar Pradesh in 1977. He is the President of the Kanpur Bar Association.
